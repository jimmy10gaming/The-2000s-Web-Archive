Sales Support

Contact Apple Store Customer Service, iTunes Store, or MobileMe Support for questions about billing, orders, or your account. Contact Apple Photo Services for questions about prints or photo books ordered through iPhoto or Aperture.

Technical Support

Online

Visit Apple’s support site for quick answers, product tutorials, self-help guides, manuals, and in-depth technical articles.

Looking for repair or service information? Use our Online Service Assistant to learn all the options for your product.

Phone

Arrange a phone call with an Apple Expert who specializes in your exact question. Talk to us now or later at your convenience. We'll even call you.

Mac, iPhone, and iPod customers within 90 days of ownership are eligible for complimentary phone support — one support incident per iPod and unlimited incidents per Mac and iPhone. Online technical support for Apple products is available beyond the initial 90 days.
